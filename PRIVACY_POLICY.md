# Privacy Policy for Coursera QuizEX

**Effective Date:** 01/12/2025

Coursera QuizEX is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension **does not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

## Information Collection

- Coursera QuizEX **does not collect any personal data**, browsing history, login credentials, or quiz content beyond what you choose to extract for your personal use.  
- All data processed by the extension (quiz questions, answers, or export files) **remains on your device** and is **never transmitted to external servers**.

## Permissions

- The extension uses the following permissions only to enable its core functionality:  
  - **activeTab**: To interact with the current Coursera page for quiz extraction.  
  - **Scripting**: To extract visible questions and answers from supported pages.  
  - **Storage**: To temporarily store quiz content and user preferences before download.  
- These permissions **do not allow access to any data beyond what you see on the page**.

## Data Sharing

- **No information collected** by this extension is shared with any third parties.

## Updates

- This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the extension. Users will be notified via the Chrome Web Store when significant updates occur.
